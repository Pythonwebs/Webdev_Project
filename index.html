The World Largest Anime Content


The world’s largest anime content platform is Crunchyroll, which offers the biggest dedicated library of anime series, movies, and simulcasts to fans worldwide. With thousands of episodes across hundreds of titles, it has become the global hub for anime streaming and distribution. Alongside this, Japan’s Anime Taizen project, created by the Association of Japanese Animations, serves as the world’s largest anime information database, archiving over 15,000 titles and nearly 180,000 episodes from 1917 to the present. Together, these platforms make anime more accessible than ever, preserving its history while delivering the widest range of content to audiences globally.
